Corporate Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 14th, 2011

1. Our Core Philosophy: Your Data is Your Property

At ENGIMO Corporation, we firmly believe that the transition to "cloud computing" is a fundamental threat to digital privacy. Our software is designed to operate completely offline. Because of this architectural choice, we inherently collect significantly less data than our competitors. What happens on your hard drive stays on your hard drive.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the information absolutely necessary to fulfill your orders and maintain your software licenses:

  • Billing and Shipping Information: When you purchase anything from our web store, we collect your name, shipping address (if applicable), and payment details via our secure 128-bit SSL encrypted checkout.
  • Account Credentials: If you register an ENGIMO ID, we store your name, username, password (we cannot view this in any way), and email address (if added).
  • Server Logs: Standard Cloudflare access logs are collected temporarily for security and debugging purposes, and are purged every 30 days.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

In stark contrast to the emerging "Software as a Service" industry, ENGIMO Corporation does not:

  • Track your mouse movements or clicks within our desktop or web applications.
  • Scan, index, or upload your personal files, photos, or video projects to remote servers.
  • Require "always-on" internet DRM check-ins to verify your license(s).
  • Sell your data to ANY third-party services except strictly necessary (such as account linking).

4. Use of "Cookies"

Our website utilizes "cookies" (small text files placed on your hard drive) strictly for maintaining your active login session in the Client Portal or Community Forums, and for storing your web store shopping cart contents. We do not use third-party tracking pixels or persistent advertising cookies.

5. Data Security

All sensitive information transmitted to ENGIMO Corporation is secured using industry-standard SSL encryption. User databases are kept on secure internal servers protected by enterprise-grade firewalls, isolated from the public-facing internet.

6. Contacting the Webmaster

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or wish to request the deletion of your forum account, please submit a ticket to the Webmaster via our Support Portal or email privacy@engimo.org.
